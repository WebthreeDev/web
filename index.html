Staking

Staking is an activity where a user locks or holds his funds in a cryptocurrency wallet to participate in maintaining the operations of a Proof-of-Stake (PoS)-based blockchain system.
It is similar to crypto mining in the sense that it helps a network achieve consensus while rewarding users who participate.
NFT staking work quiet similar to common staking but is accessible only on APR rates unlike token staking.
